Fish Preparation Process

Step 1: Whole Fish (1 KG)

The process begins with a whole fresh fish weighing about 1 kilogram. This is the starting point before any preparation.

Step 2: Chopping (700 Grams Left)

The fish is chopped into sections. After removing the head, tail, and other waste parts, around 700 grams of usable fish remains.

Step 3: Deboning

The fillet is carefully deboned by removing small bones with tweezers or a tool, making the fish safe and smooth to eat.

Step 4: Ready-to-Cook Pieces

Finally, the cleaned fillet is cut into small portions. These ready-to-cook pieces are beautifully arranged for cooking or serving.
