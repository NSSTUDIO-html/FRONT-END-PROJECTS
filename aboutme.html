About Me

I'm Naga Sasank, a passionate and creative full-stack Python developer, game designer, and founder of NS Studio TechX. I hold a B.Sc. degree in Mathematics, Statistics, and Computer Science (MSCS) and have completed a Python Full Stack Developer Course at Varshitha Computers, Ongole.

My expertise lies in Game Development, AI & Machine Learning, 3D Modeling & Animation, AR/VR experiences, and Cybersecurity. I develop innovative applications using technologies like Python, TensorFlow.js, Three.js, Blender, Termux, Django, Tailwind CSS, and Pygame.

I’ve built several original and powerful projects, including:

  • Rising Shooters – A 50-level AR action game starring 11 hero agents and an epic storyline.
  • AnimeAI – An AI animation tool that brings 3D models to life with smart motion.
  • GuardAI – A cybersecurity AI assistant that helps protect devices and online activity.
  • OneShot3D – An AI tool to convert 2D images into realistic 3D models using TensorFlow.js and Three.js.
  • Titan Core AI – A personal chatbot built with TensorFlow.js and smart interaction features.
  • AR Sketcher – An AR-based drawing assistant that overlays drawing guides in the real world.

I am dedicated to building futuristic tools and immersive experiences that combine technology, creativity, and purpose. I’m always open to collaborations, internships, and exciting development opportunities.
